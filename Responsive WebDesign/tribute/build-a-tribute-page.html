Who is him?

Zhang Hao is a Chinese singer based in South Korea and hired by Yuehua Entertainment. He became the winner of the reality show Boys Planet and debuted as a member of the South Korean boy group ZEROBASEONE in July 2023. He is known as the first foreigner to win first place in a South Korean idol survival reality show.
